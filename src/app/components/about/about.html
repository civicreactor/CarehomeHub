About Civic Reactor

At CIVIC REACTOR we have noticed that more and more is expected for less and less. As a volunteer organisation we are committed to improving access and publicly of open data resources to allow for wider utilisation and research from that data.

Civic reactors can be young, old, beginners, experts, programmers, designers, data scientists, communicators, civic organisers, entrepreneurs, employers, employees or looking for work. Anyone willing to apply themselves to solving problems and who can collaborate on co-creating solutions as a response to social, environmental and economic challenges relevant to their city are welcome.

Following the goals of the open data movement is similar to those of other "open" movements such as open source, open hardware, open content and open access and making information available and usable for all.
